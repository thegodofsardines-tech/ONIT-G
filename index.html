By 4th Grade Jonnathan excelled in physics, algebra, and geometry. However, Jonnaathan engaged in Grade 10 math physics courses, demonstrating high physics intelligence and even being top of the class despite taking the class with the grade above him. By his Grade 11 Year, Jonathan had achieved a Purple Belt in Advanced Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and had been taking Boxing, Muay Thai, and Kickboxing for striking along with Judo and Wrestling for takedowns and throws. Additionally during his grade 11 year, Jonathan kept his straight A honor roll student reputation alive, excelling in every class and course and even making time for his out of school job alongside his best friend, and community hours. However, during a late night shift at Jonathan's restaurant job with his best friend, Jonathan suddenly heard a loud crash, followed by the sound of screaming coming to an abrupt end, and then complete silence. Jonathan rushed out of the kitchen only to find his friend, dead, lying in a pool of his own blood and shards of broken glass from the destroyed window the criminal who did this came in, and out of from. From that moment on Jonathan dedicated himself to finding and bringing the justice he sought necessary to the criminal who did this, often spending hours at a time by the crime scene’s perimeter, analyzing blood splatters, glass shards spread, impact velocity, hints the criminal may have left behind on how, and why he murdered Jonnathan’s friend. Following Jonathan's Graduation from McGill University in 2013 he swiftly scored a job in Toronto’s “Violent Crime Scenes Unit”.
